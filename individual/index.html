Individual Counseling

Individual counseling provides a supportive, accepting environment to work together on your current challenges, questions, and goals. The counseling process is always tailored to your needs and can be flexibly adapted.

How can I help?

  • Stress management and reducing anxiety
  • Work-related challenges and career planning
  • Building self-confidence and deepening self-knowledge
  • Support in decision-making situations
  • Mental aspects of sports performance

If you feel that you need change or support during a more difficult period, feel free to get in touch!
