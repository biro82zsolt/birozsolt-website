Coaching

Coaching is a specific form of counseling that focuses on a solution-oriented, future-focused approach. The goal is to mobilize individual resources, increase performance, and support the achievement of personal and professional goals.

In what areas is coaching useful?

  • Career planning and professional development
  • Developing leadership skills
  • Building self-confidence and motivation
  • Supporting decision-making situations
  • Creating work-life balance

Coaching typically consists of a structured series of a few sessions, during which we work together to achieve the set goals.
