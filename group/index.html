Group Counseling

Group counseling focuses on working together in processes tailored to the needs of organizations, teams, or communities. The goal is to enhance effective cooperation, increase motivation, improve communication, and support mental well-being.

How can group counseling help?

  • Strengthening team cohesion and trust
  • Developing communication skills
  • Preventing burnout and managing stress
  • Increasing motivation and engagement
  • Supporting leadership skills

I offer trainings, workshops, and consultations for companies, sports clubs, and other groups as part of the process.
