Diagnostics and Assessment

The diagnostic process uses targeted psychological tools and structured interviews to map the current situation. Assessment helps to gain a deeper understanding, define individual and group development goals, and create a personalized intervention plan.

When is diagnostics recommended?

  • Challenges in work or sports performance
  • Identifying motivational issues
  • Assessing stress levels and burnout risks
  • Deepening self-awareness
  • Evaluating team functioning and cooperation

It is also possible to complete online questionnaires, which are evaluated during personal consultations.
